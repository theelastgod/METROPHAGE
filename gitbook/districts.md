# The Eight Districts

Eight corp holdings ring Metro City. Each has a sector designation, a distinct biome, an **ICE Vault**, and a **city-contagion weight** — the share of the Singularity meter it's worth while held. They escalate in rough order from Palantir Plaza to THE KERNEL.

## Early ground

**PALANTIR PLAZA** _(Sector 0)_ — where you boot free. A predictive-policing grid: the first district reads you before you move. Home of the campaign's opening beats and the gentlest ICE Vault, **v0**.

**ANDURIL YARDS** _(Sector 7)_ — an autonomous-drone foundry. The machines here don't need a warrant. Fast, mechanical, and the first real test of your kit.

## Mid corp tiers

**ARGUS SPIRE** _(Corporate Tier)_ — a total-awareness uplink climbing into the corporate tier. Height, sightlines, and the sense that something is always watching.

**TIDAL YARDS** _(Sector 12)_ — Blackwater freight docks. Wet, dark, and moving — cranes, containers, and the smell of laundered cargo.

**THE UNDERCITY** _(Sector 19)_ — buried metro vaults beneath the streets. Where the city keeps what it doesn't want found, including some of the coldest minds.

## Late ring

**ORBITAL RELAY** _(Uplink Tier)_ — the Skylink array, the corps' eyes in the sky. Break the relay and you cut their sightlines across the whole city.

**THE WASTELAND** _(Sector 31)_ — outer-ring salvage. The edge of the map, where the city dumps what it repossessed. The last open ground before the cage.

## The endgame

**THE KERNEL** _(The Cage)_ — the Helios master grid. This is the cage the whole system was built to defend: the deepest vault (**v7**), the final campaign beats, and **THE CUSTODIAN** waiting at the bottom. Everything before this was practice.

***

_Holding a district means holding its linked infection nodes — contagion spreads along the connections, so control is about the map, not a single point. See_ [The World → Territory & contagion](world.md#territory--contagion).
