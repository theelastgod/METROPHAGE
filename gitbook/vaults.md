# ICE Vaults & Dives

Every district hides an **ICE Vault** — an instanced, single-objective dive into a
frozen-mind storage facility. Vaults are where the campaign's memory fragments live and
where the best early gear drops.

## Anatomy of a vault

Each vault (zones **v0–v7**, one per district) runs the same authored shape, scaled to
depth:

```
  ENTRY HALL  ──►  SPLIT GUARD CHAMBERS  ──►  ANTECHAMBER  ──►  THE CORE
   (arrive)         (two garrison rooms)        (the boss)      (channel the
                                                                 fragment free)
```

At the core you **channel the fragment core** — the same channel mechanic used to hold
territory nodes — while the vault's garrison tries to stop you. Hold it, and the
frozen mind comes loose.

## Depth scaling & bosses

The deeper the vault, the harder the garrison and the better the reward.

- **v0–v1** — teaching dives; light garrisons.
- **v2 and up** — **ICE WARDEN** bosses guard the core.
- **v7 — THE KERNEL** — **THE CUSTODIAN** guards the deepest vault. This is the endgame
  dive.

## What you walk out with

- **A memory fragment** — **claim-once per player.** There are **16 authored fragments**
  to recover across the vaults; each is a piece of who you were before you were
  repossessed. Once you've claimed one, it's yours — you can re-run the vault, but the
  fragment won't drop again.
- **A guaranteed gear cache** — rarity **scales with depth**, so deeper vaults are the
  reliable path to better loot.

## THE UNDERLINE

Beneath the districts runs **THE UNDERLINE** — the subway dungeon. A separate crawl from
the vaults, threading the city's underside.
