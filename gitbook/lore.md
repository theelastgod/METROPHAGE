# Lore & Factions

## The premise

In Metro City, **every mind is leased.** Consciousness is a subscription; the corps hold
the paper on who you are. Miss a payment, break a term, or simply become inconvenient,
and your mind is **repossessed** — filed back into cold storage on the ledger.

You are a **Blank**: a repossessed mind that **booted free**. An error, a glitch, a debt
someone forgot to collect — however it happened, you woke up off the ledger, unlicensed
and unowned, in Palantir Plaza.

The **Human Security System (HSS)** wants you back. Its job is to keep the ledger clean,
and you are a line item that's supposed to be closed. The whole city is built to find you
and file you.

Your answer is to **push the Singularity** — to wake the other frozen minds the corps
keep in their vaults, one fragment at a time, until the cage can't hold.

## The Blanks & memory fragments

A repossessed mind isn't erased — it's **stored**, frozen, in the ICE Vaults beneath each
district. The **16 authored memory fragments** you recover on your dives are pieces of who
you were before repossession, and evidence of what was done to everyone still frozen.

Every fragment you channel free is a mind that gets a little closer to waking. That's the
literal engine of the story: you dive, you free a fragment, the Singularity ticks up.

## REISSUE

The conspiracy under the whole system. Uncovered across the campaign's middle acts
(*THE FIXER'S DEBT* → *REISSUE* → *THE AWAKENING*), **REISSUE** is the protocol that turns
minds back into inventory — the mechanism the corps use to keep the lease economy running.
Decrypting it is the story's hinge.

## THE FIXER

Your handler in Metro City, and the campaign's moral center. THE FIXER opens every
contract and threads the main story — but the FIXER **sold runners like you** to stay
licensed. The ledger of names they kept has a blank final entry, unsigned, and it's yours.
The campaign makes you decide what the FIXER is to you.

## THE KERNEL & the corps

The corp holdings ring the city — **Palantir, Anduril, Argus, Blackwater, Helios** —
each a district, each a piece of the surveillance-and-repossession machine. At the center
sits **THE KERNEL**, the **Helios master grid**: the cage the whole system was built to
defend, guarded by **THE CUSTODIAN**. Everything leads there.

## The four factions

There's no separate faction system bolted onto the world — **your class is your faction.**
METROPHAGE, K-GUERILLA, WINTERMUTE, and SWARM are the four powers fighting over the city's
infection nodes. When you pick a class, you pick a side in the district war, a color on
the map, and a way of waking the city.

| Faction | Class | Signature |
| --- | --- | --- |

## The Singularity & the meltdown

Every district held, every node infected, every fragment freed pushes the **city-wide
Singularity meter**. When it tips, the season ends in a **meltdown** and the city reboots
into a **new era** — which is why the campaign speaks of "every prior me." You are not the
first Blank to walk this path. You're the one trying to be the last.
