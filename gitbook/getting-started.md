# Getting Started

You wake in **Palantir Plaza** with a repossessed body and no license. Everything
after that is yours to earn.

## Controls

METROPHAGE plays like a twin-stick action-RPG: move with one hand, aim with the mouse,
and layer abilities on top.

| Input | Action |
| --- | --- |
| **WASD** / **Arrows** | Move |
| **Mouse** | Aim + fire your primary |
| **SPACE** | Dash — a short burst with **invincibility frames** |
| **Q** | Class **ability** |
| **E** | Class **ultimate** (HEAT-gated — see [Combat & HEAT](combat.md)) |
| **I** | Bag / inventory |
| **G** | Forge |
| **M** | World map |
| **J** | Quest journal |
| **H** | Surface from an interior or dive |
| **ENTER** | Area chat |
| **V** | Emotes |

**Touch devices:** tap to walk, attack, and enter; on-screen **dash / Q / E** buttons
handle abilities. The whole game is designed to run on a phone.

> **The one thing the client is allowed to do without asking:** dash. Every other burst
> of speed or damage is validated by the server, so there's nothing to exploit — just
> a game that feels responsive because it predicts locally and reconciles on the server's
> word.

## Sign in

- **Guest** — pick a callsign and play immediately. Your progress is bound to your
  device so nobody else can log in as you.
- **Wallet** — connect **MetaMask** on Robinhood Chain and sign a **free message** (no gas). This
  gives you a durable identity that survives across devices and unlocks the
  [$METRO bridge](economy.md).

## Your first hour

1. **Take the FIXER's brief.** THE FIXER in Metro City opens the campaign — *[THE WAKE](campaign.md)*.
2. **Infect some nodes.** Step into **Palantir Plaza** (district 0) and start clearing
   infection nodes. This is where you learn to move, dash, and fire under pressure.
3. **Crack your first ICE Vault (v0).** Every district has an instanced dive. Fight the
   garrison, channel the fragment core, and walk out with a **memory fragment** and a
   **gear cache**.
4. **Gear up at the Forge (G) and Market (K).** Turn your loot and credits into an
   actual build.
5. **Claim a home.** The *HOMESTEAD* interlude sends you to **THE ESTATES** — your own
   plot off the plaza.

From there the city is open: contracts, world events, bounties, the Crucible, guilds,
and eight districts' worth of territory to fight over.

```
        │                                              │
   METRO CITY  ──►  DISTRICT  ──►  ICE VAULT  ──►  LOOT + FRAGMENT
   (hub: FIXER,     (fight for     (instanced      (gear scales with
    forge, market)   nodes, run     dive boss)      depth; fragments
        ▲            events)                          are claim-once)
        │                                              │
        └──────────  spend, craft, trade, level  ◄─────┘
```

Moment to moment you're fighting. Session to session you're building a character.
Season to season the whole city tips toward the **Singularity**.
